About Us

CampusClaim is dedicated to helping students and staff recover lost items on campus. Our mission is to create a safe and efficient platform for reporting and finding lost belongings.

Founded in 2024 by a group of students at Constructor University, we understand the challenges of losing personal items in a busy campus environment. Our team is committed to providing excellent service and support to our users.
